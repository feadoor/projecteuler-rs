Problem 1 (Multiples of 3 and 5)

Solution detail

Using the inclusion-exclusion principle, we can calculate the required sum as s_3 + s_5 - s_15, where:

s_3 is the sum of the multiples of 3 below 1000.

s_5 is the sum of the multiples of 5 below 1000.

s_15 is the sum of the multiples of 15 (i.e. divisible by both 3 and 5) below 1000.

Each of these quantites is the sum of an arithmetic progression and can be calculated by the usual formula.
