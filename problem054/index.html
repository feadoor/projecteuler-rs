Problem 54 (Poker hands)

Solution Detail

There are no tricks, here; we must simply parse each pair of hands, calculate their poker value and check for a winner.

To ease comparison between two hands, they can be converted into a canonical form - a structure consisting of:

  • The type of poker hand (Straight flush, Four of a kind, Full house...)
  • The distinct ranks of cards in the hand, sorted first by count, then by value

Then lexicographic ordering of these structures corresponds to ordering of poker hands.
